About Us

We believe in second chances.

For properties, and for the families who call them home.

NextPhase Ventures is a Richmond, VA-based real estate investment company focused on fix & flip projects. We buy homes that need work, renovate them with care, and turn them into places families are excited to call home.

We started NextPhase Ventures because we saw an opportunity to do real estate investing differently — with a focus on quality renovations and a straightforward, no-pressure process for the sellers we work with.

Every property tells a story. Whether it's an inherited home, a property that's become too much to maintain, or a house that just needs the right team to bring it back to life — we approach each one with care, transparency, and respect for the people involved.

“Families deserve a place to call home.”

NextPhase Ventures is built on the belief that real estate investment can do more than build wealth — it can transform communities and change lives, one home at a time.
